Our Whitetail Habitat Plans

Customized strategies to help Southeastern Kentucky families build better deer habitat and create lasting hunting memories.

Walk-Through Plan

Perfect for landowners who want clarity on where to start. On-site property walk-through, habitat & deer movement evaluation, food plot recommendations, mineral & attraction guidance, and a clear written action plan.

🌾
Improvement Plan

For families ready to improve their property the right way. Full property walk-through & strategy session, multi-season crop & rotation planning, seasonal food plots, soil recommendations, bedding & cover improvements, stand & access strategy, and pressure & access management.

Legacy Plan

Long-term system design for multi-generational success. Comprehensive property evaluation, 3-5 year habitat blueprint, detailed multi-season crop & rotation planning, bedding & cover strategy, and pressure management tailored to your goals.

Whether you're just getting started or building a multi-generational hunting legacy, we deliver clear, actionable steps that produce real results on the ground.
