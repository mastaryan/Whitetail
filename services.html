Our Services

Specialized Whitetail Habitat Services

How We Help Kentucky Landowners Grow Bigger Bucks

🔍
Property Walk-Through & Evaluation

On-site assessment of your land’s current habitat, deer movement patterns, bedding, cover, and pressure points. Includes written recommendations and starter action plan.

🌾
Food Plot Design & Seasonal Planning

Custom multi-season food plot layouts tailored to your soil type, acreage, and Kentucky growing conditions. Includes crop rotation, planting timelines, and attraction strategies.

🌲
Bedding & Cover Improvement

Strategic creation and enhancement of bedding areas, thermal cover, and travel corridors to hold mature bucks during daylight hours and reduce pressure.

🧪
Soil Testing & Amendment Strategy

Professional soil sampling and analysis with targeted lime, fertilizer, and mineral recommendations to maximize food plot success and overall habitat quality.

🌳
Stand Placement & Low-Pressure Access

Optimized tree stand, blind, and trail camera locations combined with smart entry/exit routes to minimize pressure and maximize mature buck sightings.

🏡
Long-Term Legacy Planning

3–5+ year comprehensive habitat blueprints designed for multi-generational success, including phased implementation, budget planning, and ongoing adjustments.
